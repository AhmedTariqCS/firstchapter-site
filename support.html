Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions and select FirstChapter to manage or cancel.

How do I delete my account?

Open FirstChapter > Settings > Account > Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings > Export Data to download all your journal entries, photos, and milestones.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your memories are encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell your data or use it to train AI models. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can family members add memories?

Yes! Invite family members from the Family tab. They can view and contribute to your baby's memory book.
